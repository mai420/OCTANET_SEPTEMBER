YOUR NAME

Two teenagers share a profound, magical connection upon discovering they are swapping bodies. Things manage to become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.

PRONE TO WANDER


"Your Name" (Kimi no Na wa) is a popular Japanese animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai. Released in 2016, it became a global sensation and is known for its stunning animation, compelling story, and emotional depth.

"Your Name" tells the story of two high school students, Taki and Mitsuha, who mysteriously swap bodies. Taki lives in Tokyo, while Mitsuha resides in a rural town. The two begin communicating through notes, messages, and experiences as they navigate each other's lives. However, their connection becomes even more profound when they discover that their body-swapping is linked to a celestial event and a potential disaster

As Taki and Mitsuha search for each other and attempt to understand the nature of their connection, the film explores themes of love, destiny, and the impact of our actions on others.

The film beautifully blends elements of romance, fantasy, and drama, making it a memorable and emotionally resonant experience for viewers.

"Your Name" received critical acclaim for its animation, storytelling, and music. It became one of the highest-grossing Japanese animated films of all time and gained a dedicated fanbase worldwide. It's often considered a must-watch for anime enthusiasts and movie lovers alike.
